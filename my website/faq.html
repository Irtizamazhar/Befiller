What documents do I need to file my income tax return?
Usually, you will need your CNIC, NTN (if available), salary certificate or income details, bank statements, and information about assets or additional income sources.
How long does it take to complete the filing process?
Salaried individual filing is usually completed within 2 to 3 working days after receiving all required documents. Business and more complex cases may take longer.
Can you help salaried individuals as well as business owners?
Yes. We assist salaried individuals, freelancers, sole proprietors, partnerships, and companies with filing, registration, tax compliance, and documentation support.
Do I need an NTN before filing tax?
In many cases, tax filing is connected with NTN registration. If you are not registered yet, we can guide you through the registration process as well.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We follow professional practices for handling personal and business information securely. Your documents and financial details are treated with confidentiality.
Do you handle notices, audits, or tax-related replies?
Yes. We can assist with tax notices, documentation preparation, response drafting, and advisory support related to compliance matters.
Can you help with company registration and business compliance?
Yes. We provide support for company registration, business documentation, tax registration, and ongoing compliance-related services based on your requirements.
What if I have income from more than one source?
That is not a problem. We can handle cases where income comes from salary, business, freelancing, property, bank profit, or other eligible sources.
Can I use your tax calculator for estimate purposes?
Yes. The tax calculator is useful for quick estimated calculations. Final tax figures may vary depending on your exact financial details, applicable rules, and tax year selection.
Do you help freelancers and online earners?
Yes. If you earn through freelancing, digital services, remote work, or online business, we can guide you regarding tax filing, income reporting, and related compliance support.
What happens after I submit my documents?
Once your documents are reviewed, we organize the required information, prepare the relevant filing or documentation work, and proceed according to the selected service.
Can you help if I have never filed tax before?
Yes. First-time filers are welcome. We can guide you through the process, explain the requirements, and help you get started properly.
